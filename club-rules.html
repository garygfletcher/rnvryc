Rules of the Club

The Club Rules govern membership, administration and the conduct of the Royal Naval Volunteer Reserve Yacht Club. Upon election to membership, each member is taken to have agreed to comply with the Rules and the current Data Privacy Policy.

The controlled copy of the Club Rules is available through the members' area. Please log in to read the latest approved version.
