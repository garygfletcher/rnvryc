How to Contact Us

Our club is run entirely by volunteers. We endeavour to respond to all enquiries as quickly as possible but please accept some delays may occur, especially if the person you wish to contact is away sailing!

01

Membership enquiries

If you are thinking about joining the RNVR Yacht Club, please contact the Membership Secretary.

membership@rnvryc.org
02

Flag Officers

To contact one of the Flag Officers, email the Commodore in the first instance. Your email will be forwarded as appropriate.

commodore@rnvryc.org
03

Slops

For enquiries by suppliers regarding our Slops sales or purchases, please email the Slops Bosun.

slops@rnvryc.org
04

Anything else

Email the details and the Committee member best suited to deal with your enquiry will get back to you.

info@rnvryc.org
